Tuition & Fees

Neurodiagnostic Technology Institute is committed to making our courses affordable and attainable. We offer several tuition packages to make going back to school easier. Our tuition costs and fees are simple and straightforward.

You can choose the tuition package that best suits your needs. Tuition packages range between $9,050 to $10,855 and can include your textbooks and supplies or it can be priced à la carte.
Tuition Payment Plans

Neurodiagnostic Technology Institute is pleased to offer students the following tuition options:

 Tier #1 -
  • Cost: $9,050
  • Includes the Required Textbooks, Required Supplies, and ASET membership detailed in the Catalog
  • One payment due in whole five (5) days prior to the first class of the first term
 Tier #2 -
  • Cost: $9,550
  • Includes the Required Textbooks, Required Supplies, and ASET membership detailed in the Catalog
  • Two payments of $4,775 due five (5) days prior to the first class of the first and third terms
 Tier #3 -
  • Cost: $10,885
  • Includes the Required Textbooks, Required Supplies, and ASET membership detailed in the Catalog
  • Multiple payments equaling $10,855 paid in whole prior to graduation in accordance with the Enrollment Agreement
 Tier #4 -
  • Cost: $10,000
  • Includes Required Supplies detailed in the Catalog
  • The student is responsible for purchasing the Required Textbooks and ASET membership
  • Multiple payments equaling $10,000 paid in whole prior to graduation in accordance with the Enrollment Agreement

Textbooks and required supplies can be purchased separately for approximately $823. Student membership to ASET-The Neurodiagnostic Association is $50. The total cost does not include Board Examination fees or any other regulating agency fees. Students are encouraged to seek the best prices and purchase textbooks at their preferred location. Payment for the background check, drug screening, and immunization tracking required by clinical sites are the responsibility of the student.

Tuition Payment

Tuition for each Plan is payable no later than five days prior to the beginning of the next semester via personal check, money order, credit card, or Paypal. Checks and money orders should be made payable to Neurodiagnostic Technology Institute for the exact amount stated in your Enrollment Agreement and sent to:

               Neurodiagnostic Technology Institute
               1200 Kuhl Avenue, Suite D
               Orlando, Florida 32806

A student is not considered officially registered and may not attend classes until all fees and tuition are paid in full or other payment arrangements have been made. Neurodiagnostic Technology Institute will charge the student $50 fee for each check returned unpaid. Additionally, the student will be required to pay all future fees by money order, certified check, credit card, or Paypal.
