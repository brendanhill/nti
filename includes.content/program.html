Your first step to a new healthcare career as an EEG Technologist

Neurodiagnostic Technology Institute offers a twelve-month professional educational program that provides graduates with the knowledge and skills necessary to be eligible to seek employment as neurodiagnostic techologists and to take the EEG board exam to become Registered EEG Technologists. Our commitment extends beyond passing the board exam. The Institute helps students establish their careers by providing assistance with job search strategies, résumé preparation, and interview skills. While a job for each graduate cannot be guaranteed, each graduate will have learned the skills necessary to find one. Your success is our greatest achievement.
What you’ll learn

The Neurodiagnostic Technology Institute program helps you prepare for a rewarding career in the healthcare field of Electroencephalography (EEG). You will have unique opportunities to work with medical professionals and use your knowledge and skills to take an active role in the diagnoses and treatment patients. At the Neurodiagnostic Technology Institute you will gain the knowledge, skills and hands-on experience to operate advanced diagnostic equipment, perform neurological studies and analyze the results.
How you’ll learn

We teach using a custom blend of online and hands-on education to create an engaging and interactive learning environment. The Neurodiagnostic Technology Institute program leverages the technological advances and reach of the Internet to offer motivated students the opportunity to pursue a career in neurodiagnostic technology.

The Institute offers instructor led, virtual classroom environments that give students the flexibility to learn at home with a curriculum that meets rigorous professional education standards.
The Next Generation in Education

Imagine the advantages of a specialized professional training program that’s delivered to you wherever you are. The Neurodiagnostic Technology Institute expands the traditional classroom and puts it within your reach. If you are able to access the Internet, you can attend class.

A unique learning program that features:

  • A challenging curriculum developed by leading healthcare experts and professionals.

To provide vital hands-on experience the Neurodiagnostic Technology Institute works with neurodiagnostic facilities around the country to provide clinical site experience. It all comes down to this. Our student-friendly technology platform, interactive online classes, and clinical site experiences will help prepare you to succeed in this exciting industry.
