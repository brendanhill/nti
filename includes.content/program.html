  • A custom blend of online and hands-on education;
  • Convenient. Online learning can be completed any time, day or night. Externships are conducted at local neurodiagnostic facilities.
  • Available to be taught in all 50 states.
Your first step to a new healthcare career

Neurodiagnostic Technology Institute offers a twelve-month professional educational program that provides graduates with the knowledge and skills necessary to be eligible to seek employment as neurodiagnostic techologists and to take the EEG board exam to become Registered EEG Technologists. Our commitment extends beyond passing the board exam. The Institute helps students establish their careers by providing assistance with job search strategies, résumé preparation, and interview skills. While a job for each graduate cannot be guaranteed, each graduate will have learned the skills necessary to find one. Your success is our greatest achievement.
What you’ll learn

The Neurodiagnostic Technology Institute program helps you prepare for a rewarding career in a specialized healthcare field. You will have unique opportunities to work with medical professionals and use your knowledge and skills to take an active role in the diagnoses and treatment patients. At the Neurodiagnostic Technology Institute you will gain the knowledge, understanding, skills and experience to operate a variety of advanced diagnostic equipment, perform neurological studies and analyze the results.
How you’ll learn

We teach using a custom blend of online and hands-on education to create an engaging and interactive learning environment. The Neurodiagnostic Technology Institute program leverages the technological advances and reach of the Internet to offer motivated students the opportunity to pursue a career in neurodiagnostic technology. The Neurodiagnostic Technology Institute offers an instructor led, virtual classroom environment that gives students the flexibility to learn at home with a curriculum that meets rigorous professional education standards.
The Next Generation in Education

Imagine the advantages of a specialized professional training program that’s delivered to you wherever you are. The Neurodiagnostic Technology Institute expands the traditional classroom and puts it anywhere with an Internet connection.

An individualized learning program featuring:
