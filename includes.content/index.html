Begin an exciting new career in Neurodiagnostic Technology
Neurodiagnostic Technologists and Technicians are vital members of professional healthcare teams. They use specialized equipment to monitor how well a personâ€™s brain and nervous system are functioning. Neurodiagnostic Technologists and Technicians provide valuable data that neurologists, physicians, and surgical teams use to diagnose and treat conditions such as stroke, brain tumors, epilepsy, sleep disorders, and congenital and degenerative brain diseases.

 Neurodiagnostic Technologists/Technicians are:
  • Medical professionals
  • Science and technology oriented
  • Detail-driven
  • Effective communicators
  • Valued members of the treatment team
As a Neurodiagnostic Technologist or Technician you will be work directly with patients to collect medical histories, capture diagnostic recordings, collaborate with physicians and specialists, and maintain sophisticated medical equipment.

Begin your new career at the Neurodiagnostic Technology Institute
  • A 12-month professional, healthcare, education program
  • A custom blend of online and hands-on education
  • Externships and online learning take place concurrently
  • Courses are available in all 50 states. Externships occur locally so no travel or housing is required.
The Neurodiagnostic Technology Institute offers a twelve-month professional educational program that provides graduates with the knowledge and skills necessary to obtain work as Neurodiagnostic Technicians and be eligible to take the EEG board exam to become Registered EEG Technologists (R.
