Begin an exciting new career in Neurodiagnostic Technology

Neurodiagnostic Technologists are vital members of today’s professional healthcare teams. They use specialized equipment to monitor how well a person’s brain and nervous system are functioning. They provide valuable information that neurologists, physicians, and surgical teams use to diagnose and treat conditions such as stroke, brain tumors, epilepsy, sleep disorders, and congenital and degenerative brain diseases.

Neurodiagnostic Technologists/Technicians are:
  • Medical professionals
  • Science and technology oriented
  • Detail-driven
  • Effective communicators
  • Valued members of the treatment team

As a Neurodiagnostic Technologist you will work directly with patients to collect medical histories, capture electroencephalographic (EEG) recordings, collaborate with physicians and specialists, and maintain sophisticated medical equipment.

Begin your new career at the Neurodiagnostic Technology Institute
  • A 12-month education program in the field of Electroencephalography (EEG)
  • A custom blend of online and hands-on education
  • Externships and online learning take place concurrently
  • Courses are available nationwide. Externships occur locally so no travel or housing is required.

The Neurodiagnostic Technology Institute provides graduates with the knowledge and skills necessary to obtain work as Neurodiagnostic Technologists and be eligible to take the American Board of Registration of Electroencephalographic and Evoked Potential Technologists (ABRET) EEG board exam to become Registered EEG Technologists (R. EEG T).
