Our Privacy Policy

In this Privacy Policy, the terms “we,” “us” and “our” refers to the Virtual Learning Institute, LLC d/b/a Neurodiagnostic Technology Institute.

We appreciate the trust you place in us and recognize that it is our responsibility to keep your privacy and data secure. We do not share, rent, sell, or otherwise use any personal information you have provided us to any third party, except when required by law, subpoena or other legal process or in order to protect the safety of the public. Your personal information will not be used in ways to which you have not consented.

Our web sites will explicitly ask for information that personally identifies you to allow us to contact you. Generally, this information is requested when you have questions or would like to receive more information about the services we provide. We may disclose personal information if requested to do so by law enforcement officials or in the good-faith belief that such action is necessary to comply with applicable laws or with legal processes served on Virtual Learning Institute, Inc. If you believe that we have not adhered to this principle, please contact us by email at Info@MyNTIcareer.com and we will use reasonable efforts to promptly identify and correct the problem.

Information is automatically collected through the standard operation of our web site servers and through the use of ‘cookies.’ Cookies are small text files and are used by many popular web sites. The information we collect does not include any personally identifying information such as name or email address. We use cookies to recognize repeat users, track usage behaviors, and compile aggregate data that will provide us with a better understanding of how to improve your experience on our web site. Most internet browsers provide users with a way to disable cookies if you do not want your information collected; however, this may interfere with your ability to fully experience our web site.

Our web site contains links to other web sites which may collect your personal information. Links to these web sites are provided as a convenience and should not be interpreted as an endorsement of the linked site, its operator, or contents. Please be sure to read the policies of other web sites you visit. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of web sites that are not operated by us. The collection, use, and disclosure of your personal information by other web site operators is subject to the privacy policies provided on those web sites.

We may revise or update this policy from time to time. We recommend that you revisit this site periodically if you are concerned about how your Personal Information may be used.
