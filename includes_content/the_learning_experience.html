A new way to learn online

The online program at the Neurodiagnostic Technology Institute utilizes the cloud-based Saba learning management system. Each student has secure, password access to the Institute’s exclusive instruction website.

Inside the student portal you can create a profile, attend virtual classes and presentations, and engage in group discussions. Classes are made up of students from different locations and backgrounds that have come together online to and learn, share, and interact with instructors and each other. It’s like you’re attending a traditional educational program except you can participate from wherever you are.

The technology has been thoughtfully designed to make learning and participating simple, convenient, and intuitive. You are able to review recorded presentations; submit individual and group homework assignments; take surveys, quizzes and tests; receive instructor feedback; and access your grades at your convenience.
A new way to learn offline

The Neurodiagnostic Technology Institute is a hybrid program that combines online instruction with hands-on clinical experience. The Institute works with neurodiagnostic facilities around the country to provide clinical site experience to its students. You will receive clinical training in a neurodiagnostic lab or clinic from Registered EEG Technologists who will instruct, guide, and mentor your progress. As your knowledge and skills improve, you will be able perform tasks efficiently and effectively with minimum supervision.

Your success is our priority. When you have satisfactorily completed your program you will have the combination of knowledge, understanding, skills, and practical experience needed to take the Electroencephalography (EEG) Board Exam to become Registered EEG Technologists (R. EEG T.).
